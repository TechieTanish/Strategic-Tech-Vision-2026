### What is a Prime Number?
A prime number is a number greater than 1 that has only two factors: 1 and itself.

**Examples:**  
`2, 3, 5, 7, 11`

### Why is this important?
Prime number logic improves:
- Logical optimization
- Understanding divisibility
- Writing efficient loops

This concept is used widely in problem-solving and competitive programming.

### What is HCF?
HCF is the greatest number that divides two numbers completely without leaving a remainder.

**Example:**  
HCF of `12` and `18` is `6`

### Why learn HCF?
This helps in understanding:
- Common factors
- Mathematical relationships
- Clean and structured loop logic

It is useful in both academics and real-world problem scenarios.
